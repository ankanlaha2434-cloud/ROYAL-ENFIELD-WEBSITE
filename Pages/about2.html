About Our Legacy

Royal Enfield stands for timeless design, unmatched torque and pure riding experience. From city streets to mountain roads, it is built for riders who seek emotion, adventure and brotherhood. For more than a century Royal Enfield motorcycles have inspired riders around the world with classic styling, rugged reliability and powerful performance.

Legacy Since 1901

1901

First Royal Enfield motorcycle built in Redditch, England.

1955

Manufacturing begins in India with the legendary Bullet.

1994

Eicher Motors acquires Royal Enfield and revives the brand.

2008

The iconic Classic 350 becomes a global favorite.

2016

Royal Enfield launches the Himalayan adventure motorcycle.

2018

The revolutionary 650 twin platform is introduced worldwide.

2023

New Himalayan 450 and modern platforms expand the brand globally.

2025

Royal Enfield continues global expansion and innovation.
